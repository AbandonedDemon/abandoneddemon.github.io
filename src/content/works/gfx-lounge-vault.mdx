## Overview

{/* TODO(author): one or two paragraphs on what the platform is and who uses it. */}

A platform for running a community's GFX grading workflow end to end — collecting submissions,
scoring them against a shared rubric, ranking the results, and publishing them.

## Problem

{/* TODO(author): what was painful before this existed. */}

Grading was done by hand across multiple reviewers with no shared structure, which made scores
inconsistent and publishing slow.

## Solution

{/* TODO(author): the system you built, at a high level. */}

A structured grading pipeline with a shared 8-dimension rubric, a power-rankings editor, and a
one-click export to a Discord-ready image.

## My Contribution

{/* TODO(author): what you personally designed and built. */}

- Designed the 8-dimension scoring rubric and the multi-grader aggregation model
- Built the power-rankings editor
- Implemented DOM-to-PNG export for publishable ranking cards
- Wired up Discord publishing

## Technical Highlights

- 8-dimension grading rubric with per-grader scoring and aggregation
- Power-rankings editor for ordering and annotating results
- DOM-to-PNG export for Discord-ready ranking images
- Discord publishing integration
- Multi-grader handling with conflict-aware aggregation
